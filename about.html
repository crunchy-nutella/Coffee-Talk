About
The Project
At Germantown Friends School, students must complete a project during January of their junior year. This website is the summation of Sam Zimmer's project–he learned how to code a website and wrote articles about local coffee shops. Sam started coding in 9th grade with Python. He'd been wanting to learn other languages, so he spent his junior project taking a course by Johns Hopkins, hosted by Coursera, called "HTML, CSS, and Javascript for Web Developers." In addition to coding, Sam wanted to meet new people for his junior project. He loves coffee and he also thought that coffee shop owners were people who like meeting people, so he wanted to learn about their perspectives.
