A Note from the Designer
Hello, thank you for visiting my site. I would like to give you a small tour before you look around for yourself. The goal of the site is to introduce people to three wonderful local coffee shops in Philadelphia: Franny Lou's Porch, The Lucky Goat Coffee House, and Green Engine Coffee Co. On their respective pages, you can find the article I have written about them as well as some more ways to connect with them. The site is hosted by Github and is written in HTML5, CSS, and Javascript. Bootstrap 5 suppliments the majority self-coded styling. I am proud to say that the site is adaptable to smartphone, tablet, and desktop users. With that in mind, I encourage desktop users to change the size of their window to see how the site adapts to different screen sizes. Then, to make the site easy to navigate, there are a few ways to access the articles from the home page: the "articles" dropdown, the words in the carousel display, and the pictures of the shops at the bottom are all linked to the respective articles. Then, if you click the "about" navigation button in the header, you can see more about myself (the designer and writer), the project, the people who helped me, and the resources I used. If you are interested in seeing the code behind the website, you can click here. I hope you enjoy learning about these amazing small-business owners. Please support them by going by for a cup of coffee some time!

- Sam
